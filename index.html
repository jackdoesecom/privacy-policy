Privacy Policy

Haus Of Ecom LLC
Last updated: October 4, 2025

Haus Of Ecom LLC respects your privacy. This policy explains how our Shopify app processes data. Our app does not collect or store personal data. It temporarily accesses revenue data from Shopify APIs to calculate metrics requested by the merchant and then discards that data immediately after use.

This page is intended to satisfy Shopify App Store privacy policy requirements.

1. Scope

This policy applies to merchants who install and use our Shopify app. It also applies to any data processed through the app while delivering the requested calculations.

2. Data We Access

  • Revenue data only - totals and related values required to compute metrics.

We do not access or store customer names, emails, addresses, payment details, or other personal identifiers.

3. How We Use Data

The sole purpose of accessing revenue data is to perform calculations and display results to the merchant inside the app.

4. Retention and Deletion

  • Data is processed in real time and immediately discarded after the calculation finishes.
  • No revenue or personal data is stored on our servers.
  • Uninstalling the app stops all processing. There is no residual data to delete.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell or share data with third parties.
  • We do not use third party analytics, tracking, advertising, or logging tools.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights, never including merchant or store data processed by the app.

6. International Transfers

Data is handled through Shopify’s APIs. We do not store or transfer your data outside Shopify’s infrastructure.

7. Security

  • All API traffic uses HTTPS with modern TLS.
  • OAuth is used to control access to Shopify scopes.
  • No data is logged, cached, or persisted after use.

8. Merchant and Customer Rights

Since we do not retain data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. Merchants can uninstall the app at any time to stop processing.

9. Children

The app is for Shopify merchants and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly process data from children.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy. The effective date appears at the top. Material changes will be communicated in the app or on our listing.
